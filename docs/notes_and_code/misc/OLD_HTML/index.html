What is Ecocartographer?

Ecocartographer is a fast, user friendly computer program designed to help you find the most fuel efficient route from one destination to another. We all know about Google Maps. It uses gathered data to find the fastest route from one place to another. EcoCartographer utilizes the Google Maps platform to find the most fuel efficient route between two destinations. This allows you to save money on gas going about your everyday travels. Due to rising gas prices and alarming amounts of greenhouse gases in

the atmosphere, consumer need for saving gas has greatly increased in recent years. This could be seen in the dramatic increase of hybrid, electric, and high MPG vehicles in the past five years. EcoCartographer provides yet another way to save money and gas simultaneously by using Google Maps data to provide a fuel efficient route to your destination. EcoCartographer is a great program designed to reduce gas consumption for every route you travel.
